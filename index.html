The Educational Discount to Graduate and Excel (EDGE) is a tuition discount to recognize the academic excellence of out-of-state and international students. First-time freshman students must have a final high school unweighted GPA of 2.5 or greater to qualify for this award. Students must maintain full-time consecutive enrollment and be in good academic standing to continue receiving this tuition benefit.
